Investing.com -- iShares MSCI Brazil Capped ETF (NYSE:EWZ) fell nearly 2% after President Jair Bolsonaro tested positive for Covid-19. Bolsonaro, 65, has dismissed the coronavirus as an insignificant flu and encouraged businesses to stay open even as mayors and governors urge citizens to stay home. Brazil has the second largest number of infections in the world after the U.S., with 1.6 million testing positive and more than 65,000 deaths recorded.
