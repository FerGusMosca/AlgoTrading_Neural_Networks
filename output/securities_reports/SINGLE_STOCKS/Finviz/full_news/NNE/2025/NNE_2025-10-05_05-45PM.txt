President Trump is expected to sign executive orders on Friday that seek to jumpstart the use of nuclear energy, Reuters reports, citing sources familiar with discussions. The orders will seek to ease the regulatory process on approvals for new reactors and strengthening fuel supply chains, the report states. Publicly traded companies in the space include Oklo (OKLO), Nuscale Power (SMR), Nano Nuclear Energy (NNE), BWX Technologies (BWXT) and Constellation Energy (CEG). Published first on TheFly
