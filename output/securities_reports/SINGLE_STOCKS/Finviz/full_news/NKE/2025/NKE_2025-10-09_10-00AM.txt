When Sister  Marion Irvine,  a Dominican nun and school principal in San Rafael, Calif., decided to try jogging on Memorial Day in 1978, she didn’t own running shorts.  Sister Marion’s plan was to jog 2 miles.  Sister Marion, who died Aug. 30 at the Dominican Convent in San Rafael, Calif., at the age of 95, was a media sensation in the 1980s, featured on national TV programs and known as “the Running Nun” or “Flying Nun.”
